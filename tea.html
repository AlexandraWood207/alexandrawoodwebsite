Alex's winter herbal tea remedy

Just an easy tea recipe to share, feel free to try out for yourself!

I love this lemon and ginger tea because of its unique taste. I am left feeling revitalised by each sip! Ginger and lemon are antioxidants which fight free radicals in the body. Honey can also be used to enhance sweetness and it also has antibiotic properties, making it a reliable hot beverage during the winter months.



Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Lemon
  • Hot Water
  • Honey (optional)
  • Sugar

Preparation

  1. Add roughly one teaspoon of grated ginger in a mug. Allow water to boil in a kettle, pour hot water in the mug and allow the water to infuse for 2 minutes.
  2. Add a squeeze of lemon to the mix and place the used lemon wedge in the mug if you prefer a stronger lemon flavour.
  3. If you prefer a sweeter flavour, add either honey or sugar (or both) to taste. My prference is one teaspoon of manuka honey.
  4. Allow 2 more minutes of infusion before sipping. Enjoy!
